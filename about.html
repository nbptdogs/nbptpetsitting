About Sunset Paws

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Sunset Paws grew from a lifelong love of animals and the beautiful coastal community of Newburyport. As a local who grew up near the beach, I've always found joy in the company of dogs, especially my beloved English Cream Retrievers.

My passion for pet care and the serene beach environment inspired me to create a pet sitting service that offers more than just basic care. At Sunset Paws, we provide a unique experience that combines professional pet sitting with the relaxing atmosphere of coastal living.
