About Sunset Paws

Our Story

Sunset Paws was born out of a lifelong love for animals and the beautiful coastal community of Newburyport. As a local who grew up near the beach, I've always found joy in the company of dogs, especially my beloved English Cream Retrievers.

My passion for pet care and the serene beach environment inspired me to create a pet sitting service that offers more than just basic care. At Sunset Paws, we provide a unique experience that combines professional pet sitting with the relaxing atmosphere of coastal living.

Our Mission

At Sunset Paws, we strive to provide the highest quality pet care while incorporating the calming influence of our coastal surroundings. We believe that every pet deserves to feel loved, safe, and excited about their time with us, whether it's a walk on the beach or a cozy overnight stay.
