About Newburyport Pet Sitting

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Newburyport Pet Sitting grew from a lifelong love of animals and our beautiful coastal community. As locals who grew up near the beach, we've always found joy in the company of pets, especially dogs.

Our passion for pet care and the serene beach environment inspired us to create a pet sitting service that offers more than just basic care. At Newburyport Pet Sitting, we provide a unique experience that combines professional pet care with the relaxing atmosphere of coastal living.
